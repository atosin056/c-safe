Safety Guidelines to follow when using C-safe

Protect Personal Information:

Do not share personal information such as full names, addresses, phone numbers, email addresses, passwords, or photos without parental permission.

Privacy Settings Awareness:

Set your profile to private and restrict access to your information only to approved contacts. Familiarize yourself with the platform's privacy settings.

Safe Online Behaviors:

Log out after each session, use strong and unique passwords, and avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading unknown files.

Parental Involvement:

Parents should be actively involved in their children's online activities. Discuss any concerns or issues with them regularly.

Emergency Procedures:

In case of emergencies or if you feel threatened online, seek help from a trusted adult immediately. Know the procedures to follow if you encounter any unsafe situations.
